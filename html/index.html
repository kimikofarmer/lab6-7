Which people have favorited the song Bangerz?

How many seconds long is the longest song from a Pop album where the artist is younger than 25?

Query: SELECT MAX (length) from (song NATURAL JOIN album NATURAL JOIN artist) WHERE album.genre = 'Pop' AND artist.age < 25;

Which people whose favorite genre is Country has favorited the song Good Morning?

Query: SELECT firstname FROM (person NATURAL JOIN favorite NATURAL JOIN song) WHERE person.favoritegenre = 'Country' AND song.title='Good Morning';
